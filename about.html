The Onvo Legacy

Onvo Bath components was established in the year 1992. The Company is involved in manufacturing all types of Brass Turned Components used either for final assembly or as raw material for Electrical, Sanitary, Copper Lugs, Textile Machinery, Agricultural Machinery Components, and Antenna Components. Besides turned components, we also manufacture Brass Fittings, Compressor Fittings, Flexible Braided Hose & Fittings, Cable Glands, Brass Earthling Accessories, Brass & Mild Steel Screws, Nuts, Bolts, Washers, Moulding Inserts and any other components as per customer’s specifications.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every piece we create. Each design undergoes rigorous testing and refinement, ensuring that our products not only meet but exceed the expectations of discerning clients worldwide.

Today, Onvo continues to push boundaries in design and functionality, staying true to our founding principle: that luxury should be accessible without compromising on quality or aesthetics.
